Privacy Policy

Quick Screenshot → Clipboard does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

What the extension does

  • When you click the extension icon, it captures a screenshot of the visible area of the active tab.
  • It copies that screenshot to your device clipboard.

Data collection

This extension does not collect or transmit any user data. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Third parties

This extension does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking.
